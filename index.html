Akan Names

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Apart from the days of the week, Akan names are also given according to the gender of the person. Most people do know when their birthdays are, but some might not know what day of the week they were born.
