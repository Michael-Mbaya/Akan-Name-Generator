Akan Names

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Apart from the days of the week, Akan names are also given according to the gender of the person.

Days of the week male and female Akan names.
Day Of the Week Female Male
Sunday Akosua Kwasi
Monday Adjoa Kodjo
Tuesday Abenaa Kwabena
Wednesday Akua Kwaku
Thursday Yaa Yao
Friday Afia Kofi
Saturday Amma Kwame
